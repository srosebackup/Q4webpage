Why K-Pop Is More Complex Than You Think - by PBS Digital Studios

How we found a need of viewers. A need for viewers, in other words, is a reason or reasons why they view the content they like. To find these reasons, we performed research and collected the reasons that were the most important and common.

How did you come up with your topic?

We came up with our topic by filling out a table individually. In that table, we were asked to list down our current interests. From there, we were able to narrow it down to a common topic, which was pop music.
After choosing a topic, we narrowed it down further to make it more specific. We chose K-pop as a specific genre of pop music, since our group was interested in researching it.
