Overall performance

With our stream design and tools prepared, we promoted our stream before we could go live. We did this by uploading our promotional content or materials on social media, specifically the social media platform Facebook.

We then prepared our streaming platform by performing a test run, a DMCA checklist to ensure our streaming content complies with copyright laws, and made a copy of a survey our LF provided to receive feedback on our streaming content.

Three of our group members then performed the actual livestream on Facebook live (Samantha Ilowa, Bobbie Kalinga. Janelle Kim Domino).

After streaming, we collected the feedback from viewers and transferred the responses onto Google Sheets, Google Slides, and a Comprehensive Paper. In our Comprehensive Paper, we were assigned to document a summary of the stream, our achievements (well-done actions), areas of opportunities, level of engagement, viewership status, and the appendix.
