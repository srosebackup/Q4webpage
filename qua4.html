What were the things well done during the stream? Explain why you think they were well done.
From the feedback form responses, we believe that the stream was well done in many ways. Most viewers found our streaming topic and information presented to be new, intriguing, and relevant, with 20% rating the topic a 5 out of 5, and 40% rating it a 4 out of 5. 100% said they could use the information immediately, and 100% wanted to learn more about the topic. The stream venue and duration were also rated excellent by 100% of viewers. The overall impression of the stream was satisfactory, with most viewers (60%) rating it a 10 out of 10 while 20% rated it 9 out of 10, and 20% rated it 8 out of 10. There were also no suggestions for improvement, so we believe our stream was well done overall.

What is your level of viewership? Explain the reason why you have high or low viewership.

ccording to Facebook's view tools and analytics, our second stream had a post reach of 82, 91 3-second views, 23 1-minute views, and 3:57 average minutes viewed, while our first stream had a post reach of 54, 97 3-second views, 36 1-minute views, and 4:27 average minutes viewed.
